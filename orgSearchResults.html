Tag Words
Tag words are one or two word combinations that can be used as additional descriptors for the project. This is useful to help users find your project when searching in DOalot by giving additional information about the project. Multiple tag words should be separated with a comma. For example: planting trees, graphic design, mentoring (note: this information is optional)

Project Mission:
Enter the project's mission statement. This is a short summary of the project's goals which includes components such as values, vision, and purpose (note: this information is required)

An example of a mission statement is: 'The Universal Project is a broad-based collaborative community initiative, assisting low-income mothers and their children to help themselves complete their education and achieve economic self-sufficiency through empowerment skills, access to affordable housing, child development services, health care, support services and meaningful employment. The Universal Project helps mothers and children to develop positive self-esteem and clarify their values on which to build a successful life' (note: this project mission statement is required and is limited to 500 characters)
