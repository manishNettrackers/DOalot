Facebook Organization Link
Enter your organization’s Facebook link.  This is done
by completing the following steps:
1) Open your organization’s Facebook page
2) Copy your company’s Facebook link (from the address bar)
3) Paste this link in the ‘Facebook Link’ field on this page

Upon completing the registration process a message will be sent to the Facebook administrator who will be required to authorize the use of the Facebook account on DOalot

Note: enter a Facebook Organization Link only if YOU are the administrator of the Facebook account
